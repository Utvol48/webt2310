Pete's Pipin' Pizza

Welcome to Pete's Pipin' Pizza, where passion for pizza meets the perfect slice! Nestled in the heart of Pizzafieldville, we’ve been serving up mouthwatering, handcrafted pizzas to our community for 25 years. Our commitment to quality starts with the freshest ingredients and extends all the way to the care we put into every pie.

We believe that great pizza is all about balance—crispy, yet chewy crust, savory sauce, the finest cheeses, and fresh toppings that make every bite a flavor-packed experience. Whether you’re a fan of classic Margherita, a meat lover’s dream, or something more adventurous, we’ve got a pizza for every palate.
